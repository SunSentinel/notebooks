Current 2020 ballot initiatives

More than two dozen initiatives are making their way through Florida's petition process and could end up on the 2020 ballot. As of Oct. 2, only one has qualified for the ballot so far.

FIREARM PURCHASE BACKGROUND CHECKFloridians for Universal Background ChecksExtends the current 3-day waiting period between purchase and delivery at retail of a handgun to all purchases of all firearms. Requires that before delivery is made, the Florida Department of Law Enforcement conduct a background check on the purchaser and the seller receive a response approving the transfer of the firearm. Defines background check. Deletes current exclusion for concealed weapons permit holders and trade-ins.0

Limits or Prevents Barriers to Local Solar Electricity SupplyFloridians for Solar Choice, Inc.Limits or prevents government and electric utility imposed barriers to supplying local solar electricity. Local solar electricity supply is the non utility supply of solar generated electricity from a facility rated up to 2 megawatts to customers at the same or contiguous property as the facility. Barriers include government regulation of local solar electricity suppliers’ rates, service and territory, and unfavorable electric utility rates, charges, or terms of service imposed on local solar electricity customers.80
Marijuana Plants for Medical Marijuana PatientsPeaceful Minds for Medical MarijuanaAllows qualifying medical marijuana patients or their caregivers to grow marijuana plants for medical use. Applies only to Florida law, and does not immunize violations of federal law.2,294

This amendment applies only to Florida law and state action, and does not immunize violations of federal law.23,394

Municipal and cooperative utilities may opt into competitive markets.454,569
